(5)04. Vigilantism

1) A person who attempts to effect justice according to their own understanding of right and wrong, or an unauthorized person attempts to enforce the law. A citizen's arrest may only be effected when a civilian, out of fear for their own safety or the safety of their close friends or relatives, subdues or detains another who is violating the law.
2) A person who violates (12)10. Good Samaritan Clause.

- Penal Code (5)04 is a felony punishable by no less than 90 seconds imprisonment and no more than 120 seconds imprisonment.

NOTES: Vigilantism does not apply when a law enforcement officer in the vicinity is in need of immediate assistance, and a civilian aids such officer - for example, helping an officer apprehend an unarmed resisting suspect by holding him down.
