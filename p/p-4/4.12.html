(4)12. Obstruction Of A Government Employee

1) A person who shows a clear and motivated attempt to prevent a government employee from conducting their duties.
2) A person who fails to comply with an officer's lawful orders.
3) A person who, after being issued a ticket, citation, or infraction, continues to violate such law and ignore an officerâ€™s orders.
4) A person who enters a crime scene after being told to stop and turn away by a Peace Officer.

- Penal Code (4)12 is a misdemeanor punishable by no less than 20 minutes imprisonment and no more than 30 minutes imprisonment.

NOTES: A government employee would need to contact a peace officer to get the charge of Obstruction issued.
