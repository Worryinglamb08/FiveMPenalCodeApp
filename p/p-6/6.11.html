(6)11. Possession of Marijuana

1) A person who is found to be in possession of Marijuana shall be charged with one of the three offences depending on the amount listed below.
2) 6.0 grams or less - (6)12 Possession of Marijuana.
3) Between 7.0 grams to 27.0 grams - (6)01 Possession of a Controlled Substance.
4) 28.0 grams and more - (6)02 Possession of a Controlled Substance with Intent to Sell.

- Penal Code (6)11 is an infraction of $1,000 for possession of up to 3.0g of marijuana, $2,000 for possession of amounts greater than 3.0g but less than or equal to 5.0g, and $3,000 for possession of amounts greater than 5.0g but less than or equal to 6.0g.
