(13)09. Wiretapping Violation

1) A person who illegally conducts surveillance or wiretapping in violation of the one-party notification system without a warrant or authorization.

- Penal Code (13)09 is a felony punishable by no less than 90 minutes imprisonment and no more than 120 minutes imprisonment.

NOTES: Cops can charge this but usually a judge is the one who notes or acknowledges what is an illegal wiretapping operation. One-party notification means that at least one party being recorded visually or audibly is aware that they are being recorded and consents to it.
