(2)03. Trespassing within a Restricted Zone

1) A person who, without proper authorization, enters any government owned or managed facility, or restricted section in a government building that is secured with the intent of keeping non-authorized personnel out due to a security or safety hazard.
2) This charge cannot stack with (2)02. Trespassing

- Penal Code (2)03 is a misdemeanor punishable by no less than 30 minutes imprisonment and no more than 50 minutes imprisonment. This falls under Officer Discretion

NOTES: As an example: areas of a police station that are restricted (i.e. armory, locker rooms, offices), trespassing inside the restricted areas of the Correctional Facility, trespassing inside restricted areas of a hospital (i.e. staff area of the pharmacy, surgery theatres when not permitted).
