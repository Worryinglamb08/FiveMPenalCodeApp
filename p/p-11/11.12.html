(11)12. Registration Violation

1) A person driving a vehicle on a state, county, or local road without an official owner’s registration or lease registration on file or in hand, or an individual who operates a motor vehicle with no valid license plates on a public roadway.

- Road Law (11)12 is an infraction of $5000, as well as a driver’s warning or impoundment of the individual’s vehicle at the peace officer’s discretion.
