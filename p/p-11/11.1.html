(11)01. Speeding Violation

1) Speeding (Stopped in The Roadway)
2) Speeding 15-25 kmh slower than the posted speed limit.

3) Speeding 10-15 kmh over the posted/known speed limit.
4) Speeding 20-25 kmh faster than the posted/known speed limit
5)Speed 25-30 kmh faster than the posted/known speed limitn
6) Speed 25-40 kmh faster than the posted/known speed limit
7)Speed 40+ kmh faster than the posted/known speed limit
8)Speeding double or 100+ the posted/known speed limit. Whichever comes first.

- Road Law (11)01 I. is an infraction punishable by a $65 fine.
- Road Law (11)01 II. is an infraction punishable by a $65 fine.
- Road Law (11)01 III. is an infraction punishable by a $170 fine.
- Road Law (11)01 IV. is an infraction punishable by a $234 fine.
- Road Law (11)01 V. is an infraction punishable by a $350 fine.
- Road Law (11)01 VI is an infraction punishable by a $475 fine.
- Road Law (11)01 VII. is an infraction punishable by a $550 fine.
- Road Law (11)01 VIII is a felony punishable by a $1000 fine, imprisonment of no less than 25 minutes and no more than 40 minutes, as well as revoking of license for 3 months.
