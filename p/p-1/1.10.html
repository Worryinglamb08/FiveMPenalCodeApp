(1)10. Murder

1) A person who unlawfully kills another with malice aforethought.
2) A person who commits murder while engaging in a felony offense that has been proven to be a premeditated act. \n\n - Penal Code (1)10 is a felony punishable by no less than 510 minutes imprisonment and no more than 660 minutes imprisonment.
