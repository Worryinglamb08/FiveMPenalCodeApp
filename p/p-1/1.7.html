(1)07. Aggravated Battery

1) A person who uses great or continued force or violence against another person and causes severe harm.
2) A person that uses a weapon, tool or other dangerous item to cause severe harm to a person(s).

- Penal Code (1)07 is a felony punishable by imprisonment of no less than 60 minutes and no more than 240 minutes.
- If a weapon is used and severe harm is inflicted on a person(s), the perpetrator shall receive the maximum sentence.
