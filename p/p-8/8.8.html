(8)08. Hit And Run

1) A person who hits another person or occupied vehicle and leaves the scene of the accident.
2) A person involved in the accident who after being requested by another party involved in the accident fails to disclose their name, provide their license for observation for the purpose of identifying such person, or provides false and misleading information.

- Penal Code (8)08 is a felony punishable by 180 seconds of imprisonment.

NOTES: This charge can be applied for each instance of hit and run occurring over the course of a road law incident.
